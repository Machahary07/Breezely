Platform Overview

Engineered for speed, efficiency, and intelligence.

Agentic Reasoning

Every command you give Breezely is parsed through a multi-modal reasoning engine. It doesn't just guess; it understands the DOM, identifies relevant elements, and plans its sequence of actions before execution begins.

Cloud Orchestration

Offload complex workflows to our cloud. Breezely uses distributed workers to handle long-running browser tasks, state management, and multi-tab automation without draining your local system resources.

Native Integration

Breezely integrates directly into your browser's context. By understanding your open tabs and active windows, it bridges the gap between different web tools, allowing for seamless cross-application automation.
